# 🎤 Reflection – Mock Interview Experience

## STAR Method Reflection

---

### ⭐ Situation

As part of my work readiness training for PRP370S, I was required to
record a mock interview and embed it in my digital portfolio. I had
never recorded a formal interview before and felt nervous about
presenting myself professionally on camera.

---

### 🎯 Task

My task was to prepare answers to common interview questions, record
myself answering them confidently, and embed the video into my
GitHub portfolio using Markdown.

---

### ⚙️ Action

To complete this task, I:
- Researched common ICT job interview questions
- Practised my answers by writing bullet points for each question
- Recorded multiple takes until I was satisfied with my delivery
- Reflected on my body language, tone, and content

---

### ✅ Result

The experience taught me that interview preparation is a skill that
requires practice. I identified areas where I need to improve, such
as maintaining eye contact with the camera and speaking more slowly.
However, I also gained confidence in articulating my technical skills
and academic achievements. I now feel better prepared for real job
interviews in the ICT industry.
